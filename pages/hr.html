HR & People Rules
KNL-HR-001 · Last reviewed April 2026

This section explains how we hire people, what we expect from everyone while they work here, and what happens when someone leaves. Our goal is a team that is skilled, honest, and safe for everyone.

Hiring — how we choose new people

  • We hire based on skills, experience, and how well someone fits our values. We do not discriminate.
  • Before you start, we run a background check. This is normal and protects our clients and team.
  • You will sign three agreements: (1) a confidentiality agreement — you keep our secrets, (2) an IP agreement — work you create for us belongs to us, and (3) an AI ethics agreement — you promise to use AI tools responsibly.
  • If you are a contractor or vendor, you sign similar documents before any work begins.

Using AI tools at work

  • Only use AI tools that KNEURALABS has approved in writing. Ask your manager for the current approved list.
  • Never paste client information into public AI tools like ChatGPT, Google Gemini, or similar free tools. These tools may use your input to train their models, which means private client data could leak.
  • Always double-check AI outputs before using them. AI makes mistakes. You are responsible for the final result.
  • If you find a useful AI tool that is not on the approved list, ask your manager to get it reviewed — do not just start using it.

Conflicts of interest — when your personal life and work overlap

  • A "conflict of interest" means a situation where your personal interests could affect your work decisions unfairly.
  • Example: If a company you invest in wants to become our client, that's a conflict. You should tell your manager so someone else can handle that client relationship.
  • Always disclose conflicts early. Hiding them is worse than having them.

When you leave KNEURALABS

  • Return everything: laptop, phone, access badges, and any other company equipment on your last day.
  • Your accounts and access will be disabled within 24 hours of your last day.
  • Your duty to keep company secrets does not end when you leave — it lasts forever. You cannot take client lists, project files, or confidential documents with you.
  • If you are unsure what you can and cannot take, ask HR before your last day.

Do

  • Finish AI & data training in your first 5 days.
  • Use only approved AI tools — check the list.
  • Tell your manager early about any conflict of interest.
  • Return all company gear on your last day.
  • Ask before you assume — questions are welcome.

Don't

  • Paste client info into ChatGPT, Gemini, or any public AI.
  • Use AI tools that aren't on the approved list.
  • Trust AI output blindly — always check it.
  • Take client lists or files when you leave.
  • Hide a conflict of interest — disclose it.
For HR vendors and partners: Keep your security certifications current, send monthly HR reports, and notify us within 24 hours of any data breach involving employee information.
