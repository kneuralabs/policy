HR & People Rules
KNL-HR-001
  • Hiring: We choose people based on skill and fit. We run background checks and you must sign agreements about keeping secrets, owning your work, and using AI ethically.
  • Training: Within your first 5 days, finish training on AI safety and protecting private information.
  • Using AI tools: Only use AI tools that the company approves. Never paste client data into public AI like ChatGPT. Always double-check AI results.
  • How to act: Be honest, protect data, and tell your manager if you have a personal conflict with a client or project.
  • Leaving the company: Return all laptops, phones, and badges. Your access will be turned off within 24 hours. Your duty to keep secrets never ends.
Our HR partner must keep security certificates, send monthly reports, and tell us within 24 hours if any data breach happens.
