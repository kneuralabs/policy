Workplace & IT Rules
KNL-WP-001
  • Remote work: Use a secure home Wi-Fi, lock your computer when away, and never work with client data in public coffee shops. Follow the same security rules as in the office.
  • Acceptable use: Company devices are for business. No hacking, no pirated software, no running a side business. You have no expectation of privacy on company devices.
  • Physical security: Lock office doors, wear your ID badge, and don't let strangers follow you inside. Clean your desk — don't leave sensitive papers out.
  • Records retention: Keep documents as long as the law requires (e.g., 7 years for financial records). Securely delete them when no longer needed.
