Workplace Conduct
KNL-ETH-001
  • Ethics: Always act honestly. Tell your manager if you have a conflict of interest. Do not accept gifts worth more than $50 without approval.
  • Equal opportunity: We do not discriminate based on race, gender, age, disability, or other protected traits. We provide reasonable accommodations.
  • Anti-bribery: No bribes, kickbacks, or "facilitation payments" ever. Follow the US Foreign Corrupt Practices Act (FCPA).
  • Reporting violations: Report misconduct to our legal partner. We do not tolerate retaliation against anyone who reports in good faith.
