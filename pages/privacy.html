Data Privacy
KNL-DAT-001
  • Basic principles: Collect only what we need, tell people why we collect it, keep data accurate, and protect it with strong security.
  • Your rights: You can ask to see, correct, or delete your personal data. We must answer within 30 days.
  • Data breach: Report any possible breach to leadership within 2 hours. We will notify regulators within 72 hours if required by law.
  • Sending data across borders: Personal data from Europe cannot leave Europe without special safeguards (SCCs).
Any vendor that handles personal data must sign a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) with us.
