Safe Workplace
KNL-SAFE-001
  • Sexual harassment: Zero tolerance. Unwanted touching, sexual jokes, or offensive comments are forbidden. Report to HR or a manager. We investigate within 15 days.
  • Workplace violence: No threats, bullying, or physical aggression. Report any incident immediately.
  • Drugs & alcohol: No illegal drugs. No drinking during work hours unless it's a company-approved event. Never come to work impaired.
  • No retaliation: If you report a problem in good faith, you are protected.
