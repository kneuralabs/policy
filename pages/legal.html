Legal & AI Rules
KNL-LEG-001
  • EU AI Act: We classify AI systems by risk: Unacceptable (banned), High (extra checks), Limited, or Minimal risk.
  • NIST AI framework: We follow four steps: Govern, Map, Measure, Manage.
  • Privacy laws (GDPR, CTDPA): If a person asks to see or delete their data, we must respond within 30 days.
  • Contracts: Never start work without a signed agreement. All work you create belongs to KNEURALABS.
Our legal partner must be licensed in Connecticut, track new AI laws, give quarterly reports, and have professional insurance.
