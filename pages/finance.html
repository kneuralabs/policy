Finance & Expenses
KNL-FIN-001
  • Getting approvals: Up to $500 → ask CEO or MD. $500–$5,000 → same with a note. $5k–$25k → both CEO and MD. Over $25k → both must agree and write a reason.
  • Sending invoices: Send within 5 days after finishing work. Payment due in 30 days. Include contract or purchase order number.
  • Expense claims: Submit receipts within 15 days. We don't pay for personal items or entertainment over $150.
  • Fraud: If you suspect cheating or stealing, tell the CEO or MD immediately. The law protects whistleblowers.
Our finance partner keeps audit logs, provides monthly financial reports by the 10th, and stores records for 7 years.
