KNEURALABS Policies
KNL-000

We help companies use AI responsibly. These rules apply to everyone: employees, contractors, and partners.

What you must do: Read and follow these rules. Ask your manager or the CEO if something is unclear.
  • Who is in charge: Piyal Gupta (CEO) and Piyali Dhar (Managing Director).
  • Updates: We review these rules every year. Next update: April 1, 2026.
  • Outside helpers: Any vendor or freelancer must follow the same rules as our employees.
  • Breaking rules: If you don't follow these rules, you may face discipline, lose your contract, or face legal action.
If you see something wrong, speak up. We protect people who report in good faith.
